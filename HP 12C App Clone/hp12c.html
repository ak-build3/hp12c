HP-12C replica

A full working clone of the HP-12C financial calculator (RPN).

f (gold key) then a key runs the gold function printed above it. g (blue key) then a key runs the blue function on its lower face.

Example — monthly payment on a $250,000 loan, 30 years at 6.5%:
f CLEAR-FIN · 30 g 12× · 6.5 g 12÷ · 250000 PV · 0 FV · PMT

Everything works: TVM, f AMORT, NPV/IRR cash flows, bonds (f PRICE / f YTM), depreciation (SL/SOYD/DB), date arithmetic, statistics, STO/RCL registers, and keystroke programming (f P/R). Settings and programs are saved on your device.

Install on iPhone: open this page in Safari → Share ⎓ → Add to Home Screen. Hold the phone sideways.
